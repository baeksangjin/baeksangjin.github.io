    Hello, I am Sangjin Baek, a product designer based in Seoul. Having navigated through cloud9, d’strict, and designfever, I currently design product experiences for content services at NAVER. I believe in the profound power of precise order and the art of subtraction, focusing on sharpening the user experience through a restraint that preserves the core essence over ornamentation. My work transcends mere visual experimentation or abstract digital work; it is dedicated to the pivotal moment where data and logic translate into tangible UX/UI products that deliver unexpected joy and vivid sensations. By stripping away unnecessary weight and architecting systems where fundamental curiosity thrives—this is how I communicate with the world through design.
